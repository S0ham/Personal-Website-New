The MF Sales
Recommendation Engine

How I turned a slow, manual sales bottleneck into a tool that produces client-ready mutual fund recommendation documents in minutes — with live market data and locked-in brand quality.

Role
Concept, build & rollout
Context
Wealth management sales team
Type
Internal AI tool
Status
Live, in daily use
The problem

Every recommendation was a mini project.

When a sales executive wanted to send a client a mutual fund recommendation, the process looked like this: look up each fund manually, copy performance numbers into a document, format everything to brand standards, get it checked, fix the formatting again, send.

It was slow, error-prone, and inconsistent. Every document looked slightly different. Data could be outdated by the time it reached the client. And the team's time went into formatting — not selling.

"The sales team shouldn't be fighting formatting. They should be talking to clients."
— the insight that started it
The solution

One input.
One share-ready document.

I designed and built an AI-powered tool that takes three things — the client's name, a shortlist of funds, and rough reasoning — and handles everything else automatically.

STEP 01
Client details in
The executive enters the client's name, the shortlisted funds, and quick rationale — no formatting, no research prep.
STEP 02
Live data fetch
The tool pulls current fund data — NAV, returns, ratings, portfolio details — from live public sources like AMFI and Value Research.
STEP 03
Brand assembly
Everything is composed into a locked, professionally branded document template — colors, typography and layout always on-brand.
STEP 04
Share-ready out
A polished, client-ready recommendation document — ready to share digitally or print to PDF. Minutes, not days.

Why this project matters

Marketing that ships product.
