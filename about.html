About me

Not here to
fit the mold.

I'm a 24-year-old marketing generalist based in West Bengal, India. I lead marketing at Dhanvesttor, consult independently for Bathwal Corporation, and I'm chasing an MBA on the side.

I treat everything I work on as if it has my name on it — because in my head, it always does. Whether it's a campaign, a deck, an automation workflow, or a brochure: I build it to last.

I thrive at the intersection of creativity and strategy. I bring structure to chaos and personality to systems. I'm equally comfortable writing a financial content piece and prototyping a full marketing automation stack.

How I operate

01

Ownership mindset

I build brands, not just campaigns. Every touchpoint adds up to a narrative. I think in systems, not one-offs — and I treat every client's brand like my own.

02

03

Market intelligence

Markets never stop shifting. You need someone tracking the shift in real time and translating it into action. Finance, politics, economics, AI — I read widely and apply laterally.

04

Problem solver

My core belief: every problem has a solution. I don't bring problems to the table — I bring options, frameworks, and the energy to execute.
